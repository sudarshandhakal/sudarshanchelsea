Hazard is a Belgium international, having represented his nation at under-17 and under-19 level.
Hazard made his senior international debut in November 2008, aged 17, in a friendly match against
Luxembourg. Nearly three years after his debut, Hazard scored his first international goal against
Kazakhstan in October 2011. He has since earned 76 caps, and was a member of the Belgian squad which
reached the quarter-finals of the 2014 FIFA World Cup and the UEFA Euro 2016.
